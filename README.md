# Awesome Game Design 

A curated collection of game design resources for designers, developers, students, and anyone interested in creating better games.

The goal of this repository is to gather high-quality resources in a single place, including books, articles, talks, tools, research papers, templates, communities, and more.

## Categories

## 📚 Books

### Game Design
- [**Fundamentals of Game Design**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Fundamentals+of+Game+Design+Ernest+Adams) — Ernest Adams (2010). The most widely cited reference in design courses, covering everything from initial concept to final balancing.
- [**Game Mechanics: Advanced Game Design**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Game+Mechanics+Advanced+Game+Design+Adams+Dormans) — Ernest Adams & Joris Dormans (2012). How to model and visualize a game's internal economy before writing a single line of code.
- [**A Game Design Vocabulary**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=A+Game+Design+Vocabulary+Anna+Anthropy) — Anna Anthropy & Naomi Clark (2014). Proposes a shared language for talking about design: verbs, challenges, and feedback loops.
- [**Patterns in Game Design**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Patterns+in+Game+Design+Bjork+Holopainen) — Staffan Björk & Jussi Holopainen (2005). A catalog of reusable gameplay patterns that cut across genres.
- [**Challenges for Game Designers**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Challenges+for+Game+Designers+Brathwaite+Schreiber) — Brenda Brathwaite & Ian Schreiber (2009). Non-digital exercises to train your design judgment.
- [**Game Design Theory**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Game+Design+Theory+Keith+Burgun) — Keith Burgun (2012). An attempt to formally define what separates a game from other interactive systems.
- [**Designing Virtual Worlds**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Designing+Virtual+Worlds+Richard+Bartle) — Richard Bartle (2003). A historical reference on persistent world design, from MUDs to MMORPGs.

### Level Design
- [**Level Design: In Pursuit of Better Levels**](https://www.edgargomez.io/2024/05/14/free-ebook-level-design-in-pursuit-of-better-levels/) — Tychobolt. A free ebook focused on the level planning process, including multiplayer map design.
- [**A Practical Guide to Level Design**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=A+Practical+Guide+to+Level+Design+Benjamin+Bauer) — Benjamin Bauer (2023). Written from AAA experience, covering both production and the soft skills the role demands.
- [**Level Design for Games: Creating Compelling Experiences**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Level+Design+for+Games+Creating+Compelling+Experiences+Phil+Co) — Phil Co (2004). A classic guide walking through the full process, from preproduction to a finished level.

### Narrative Design
- [**Game Writing: Narrative Skills for Videogames**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Game+Writing+Narrative+Skills+for+Videogames+Chris+Bateman) — Chris Bateman (ed.) (2021). A collection of professional game writers on nonlinear narrative and structure.
- [**Storytelling for Virtual Reality**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Storytelling+for+Virtual+Reality+John+Bucher) — John Bucher (2017). How to adapt classical narrative structure to immersive spaces.

### Production & Pipeline
- [**Game Development and Production**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Game+Development+and+Production+Erik+Bethke) — Erik Bethke (2003). Team management, task tracking, and technical production documentation.
- [**Extreme Programming Explained**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Extreme+Programming+Explained+Kent+Beck) — Kent Beck & Cynthia Andres (2004). The foundation of agile development, applicable to creative production teams.
- [**Managing Virtual Teams**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Managing+Virtual+Teams+Brown+Huettner) — M. Katherine Brown et al. (2007). Communication and collaboration tools for distributed teams.

### Game Studies & Culture
- [**Cybertext: Perspectives on Ergodic Literature**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Cybertext+Perspectives+on+Ergodic+Literature+Espen+Aarseth) — Espen Aarseth (1997). A foundational text of game studies on ergodic literature.
- [**How to Talk About Videogames**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=How+to+Talk+About+Videogames+Ian+Bogost) — Ian Bogost (2015). Game criticism beyond the language of film.
- [**Persuasive Games**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Persuasive+Games+Ian+Bogost) — Ian Bogost (2007). How games argue through their rules, not just their narrative.
- [**Rise of the Videogame Zinesters**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Rise+of+the+Videogame+Zinesters+Anna+Anthropy) — Anna Anthropy (2012). A manifesto on game design outside the traditional industry.
- [**Extra Lives: Why Video Games Matter**](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Extra+Lives+Why+Video+Games+Matter+Tom+Bissell) — Tom Bissell (2010). A personal essay on why video games matter as an art form.
